RECIPE NO.01

EGG GRAVY


STEP01: Add oil to a vessel
STEP02: 1Tsp jeera
STEP03: 2 dry chilli
STEP04: 2 Cloves
STEP05: 1 Cardamom
STEP06: 1/2 Bay leaf
STEP07: 1 small cinnamon stick
---Roast well---
STEP08: 6 pieces of garlic
STEP09: 2 chopped onions
STEP10: 1Tsp salt + turmeric powder
---Saute it---
STEP11: 1 chopped tomato
---it should be a bit dry---
STEP12: 1Tsp coriander powder + chlli powder
---now it should become fully dry---
STEP13: Add water and let it cook for 2mins (until it becomes a semi-gravy)
STEP14: Add boiled eggs or just break the eggs
STEP15: 1/2Tsp of pepper powder
STEP16: Add more water
---Let it cook for 5mins---
---check salt and add coriander----
-------READY!!!!--------
